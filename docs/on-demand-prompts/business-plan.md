# Business Plan Snapshot Prompt

Use this when a dated business plan is needed.

## Instructions

- Read `HANDOFF.md`, relevant TODO items, relevant ADRs, and any external research links supplied by the user.
- Do not infer market facts, competitive claims, or business viability from code alone.
- If evidence is missing, add an `Evidence Needed` section instead of inventing.
- Write the result to `docs/snapshots/business-plan-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
- Add a header that says the document is a snapshot and not a live source.

## Include

- purpose of the document
- current product state
- target segment
- problem statement
- business model or monetization hypothesis
- competitive or market evidence
- assumptions and evidence gaps
- next approval or decision needed
